THE CURRICULUM

7 Modules. Zero Fluff.

Each module builds on the last. By the end, you'll have a complete facilitation system and the business skills to actually make money with it.

MODULE 1

Why Facilitation Changes Everything

  • Why collaboration is inherently messy (and will never change)
  • The facilitator's real job: working WITH the chaos, not against it
  • What meetings actually look like vs. what people pretend they look like
  • Why companies pay $10k-$50k/day for great facilitators
  • "People support what they help create" and what that means for you
MODULE 2

The 4 Principles That Make Every Session Work

  • Discussions are Sequenced: controlling who speaks, when, and for how long
  • Discussions are Visualized: building a real-time map of the conversation
  • We Work Alone Together: why individual work in group settings produces better ideas
  • There's Always a Decision: ending every session with clear next steps and owners
  • How AJ&Smart runs every internal meeting using these principles
MODULE 3

Becoming an Unshakeable Facilitator

  • Priming: how to get into the right state BEFORE the session starts
  • The 3 mindsets: Optimism, Adaptability, and Awareness
  • How these combine into "Unshakeability," the core ECS mindset
  • Attacks on your optimism: frustration, tiredness, negativity, and how to handle each
  • Why facilitators are service people, not consultants or stars
MODULE 4

The 6 Core Skills of a Facilitator

  • Neutrality, Inclusivity, Questioning, Energy Management, Active Listening, Clear Instructions
  • Dealing with 5 types of difficult participants (and what to do about each)
  • The Parking Lot technique for keeping sessions on track
  • Social contracts: getting buy-in before things go sideways
  • The Iceberg: what clients see vs. what you're actually doing
MODULE 5

The 4C's: The Structure Behind Every Great Workshop

  • Collect, Choose, Create, Commit: the universal framework for any session
  • Why normal meetings fail (everyone collecting, choosing, and creating simultaneously)
  • How to use 4C's as a mental map, not a rigid agenda
  • The emotional arc of a great workshop and how to ride it
  • Adapting emphasis by client type: startups vs. enterprise vs. non-profits
MODULE 6

The Toolkit: Exercises, Activities, and Workshop Recipes

  • The 4 core activities: Post-ups, Clustering, Voting, and Prioritization
  • 3 types of voting and when to use each (heat map, straw poll, decider)
  • Ready-to-run recipes: Lightning Decision Jam, Strategy Signal, Design Sprint
  • How to combine exercises into custom workshop formats
  • "You'll never run out of exercises. The challenge is always the mindset."
MODULE 7

Building Your Facilitation Business

  • Pricing workshops at $5k-$15k+ (price decisions, not time)
  • The math: 6 workshops/year at $10k = $60k for 12 days of work
  • From order-taker to strategic partner positioning
  • Finding clients and writing proposals that close
  • Productizing your facilitation for recurring revenue
