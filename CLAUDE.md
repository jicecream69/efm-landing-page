## Company
**Facilitator.com** by AJ&Smart. Founded 2010 in Berlin. 35,000+ people trained, 200+ global companies (Google, LEGO, P&G, etc.). Founder: Jonathan Courtney (from Ireland, based in Berlin). The proprietary methodology is called ECS (Emergent Collaboration System).

## Product
Single long-form sales page for **Emergent Facilitation Mastery**, a self-paced online facilitation training program. Two tiers: **Core ($1,997)** and **Pro ($2,997)**. Pro adds weekly coaching calls, Skool community, and live events. Always-open enrollment, 100% virtual. See `EFM-PRODUCT-SPEC.md` for full product details, module breakdown, competitive positioning, and content analysis.

## Language Rules
- **Certificate of completion**, never "certification"
- Tiers are "Core" and "Pro"
- No mention of in-person programs on this page
- Always-open enrollment, no scarcity/deadline messaging
- Never use emdashes
- CTA language: "Enroll Now" / "Start Today"
- Hero headline is exact: "Become the most valuable person on every project."
